10 Feet Sliding Doors - Features & Benefits

10 feet sliding doors comprise of two or more frames that can slide horizontally, to the left or right on tracks. They operate smoothly since they are slightly raised above the track, preventing dust from settling in the track.

  • Nylon rollers in the bottom to ensure smooth movement
  • Touch lock feature for easy locking
  • Multi chambered for extra strength and better thermal conductivity
  • More opening for better sunlight and natural air circulation
  • Rain track for a free-flow drain system
  • Hurricane bar option to withstand heavy storms and weather
  • Superior soundproofing capabilities
  • Variety of design, colors and hardware options

10 feet Sliding UPVC doors are a perfect fit if you are looking to make your space look well-lit and spacious.
