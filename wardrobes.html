Sliding Door Wardrobe

Sleek. Space-Saving. Stylish.

Sliding wardrobes are the perfect choice for modern compact homes. With gliding doors that don't swing out, they save space while offering a streamlined look.

Layout Type: Sliding Door
Ideal For: Small to mid-size bedrooms, studio apartments

Storage:

  • Full-height hanging space
  • Internal drawers for organized essentials
  • Pull-down hanger rods
  • Overhead loft units
  • Built-in mirror panel or glass shutters
Best Fit For: Rooms with limited clearance space. The smooth-glide mechanism makes it ideal for urban apartments or bedrooms with tight walkways.

Walk-In Wardrobe

Luxurious. Spacious. Boutique-Style.

Turn your dressing space into a private retreat with a walk-in wardrobe. Designed for larger rooms or master bedrooms, this layout delivers unmatched organization and grandeur.

Layout Type: Walk-In / U-Shaped / Gallery Style
Ideal For: Villas, master bedrooms, luxury apartments

Storage:

  • Open shelving with spotlight
  • Drawer islands for accessories
  • Shoe racks, tie holders, belt trays
  • Mirror wall and vanity station
  • LED-lit hanging rods
Best Fit For: Homeowners who desire a boutique wardrobe experience at home. Ideal if you want separate zones for him and her.

Corner Wardrobe

Smart. Functional. Space-Maximizing.

Corner wardrobes make clever use of otherwise under-utilized space. Perfect for compact bedrooms or layouts with awkward corners.

Layout Type: L-Shaped or Angular Corner
Ideal For: Compact bedrooms, utility rooms, shared kids' rooms

Storage:

  • Corner carousel shelving
  • Hanging + folded combo zones
  • Modular drawers with dividers
  • Overhead cabinets for suitcases or blankets
  • Mirror-integrated corner doors
Best Fit For: Homeowners looking to make use of every inch without compromising design.

Floor-to-Ceiling Wardrobe

Seamless. Grand. Built-In Feel.

These wardrobes stretch from floor to ceiling, giving you a sleek, custom-fitted look and maximizing vertical storage like never before.

Layout Type: Full-Height Panel Wardrobe
Ideal For: Bedrooms with high ceilings, urban flats

Storage:

  • Double-decker hanging zones
  • Loft units for luggage

  • Hidden internal mirrors
  • Push-to-open or handleless shutters
Best Fit For: People who love a clutter-free aesthetic with ample concealed storage in one seamless frame.
