Why Choose WPC Doors Over Plywood Doors in 2023 ? – Space Designs

When it comes to choosing the right doors for your home or office, several factors need to be considered, such as installation, price, and durability. In recent years, WPC (Wood Plastic Composite) doors have gained popularity as a best alternative to traditional plywood doors. In this article, we will dig deep into the reasons why WPC doors are the better choice,

Installation Convenience

Installing WPC doors is significantly easier and quicker compared to plywood doors. WPC doors are pre-fabricated and come in standard sizes, making installation a hassle-free process. They can be easily cut, drilled, and fixed, reducing both time and effort during the installation process. On the other hand, plywood doors require extensive labor and expertise to measure, cut, and fit accurately, increasing installation time and costs.

Price and Affordability

WPC doors offer excellent value for money when compared to plywood doors. While the initial cost of WPC doors may be slightly higher, their long-term benefits make them a cost-effective investment. WPC doors are highly resistant to moisture, termites, and decay, eliminating the need for regular maintenance, repair, or replacement. In contrast, plywood doors are susceptible to moisture damage and require periodic treatments, adding to the overall cost of ownership.

Durability and Strength

WPC doors are known for their exceptional durability and strength. They are manufactured by combining wood fibers or sawdust with thermoplastic polymers, resulting in a composite material that possesses the best features of both wood and plastic. This composition makes WPC doors highly resistant to rot, warping, cracking, and termite attacks, ensuring a longer lifespan compared to plywood doors.

Moisture Resistance

One of the significant advantages of WPC doors over plywood doors is their excellent resistance to moisture. Plywood doors are prone to swelling, warping, and delamination when exposed to humidity or water. In contrast, WPC doors are engineered to be waterproof and do not absorb moisture, making them suitable for areas prone to high humidity, such as bathrooms and kitchens. Their resistance to moisture also prevents the growth of mold and mildew, ensuring a healthier living environment.

Environmental Sustainability

WPC doors are considered more environmentally friendly compared to plywood doors. The manufacturing process of WPC doors involves recycling wood waste and plastic materials, reducing the demand for virgin timber and plastic. By using recycled materials, WPC doors help conserve natural resources and reduce environmental pollution. Additionally, since WPC doors have a longer lifespan and require less maintenance, they contribute to a lower carbon footprint over time.

Aesthetics and Customisation

WPC doors offer a wide range of design options and finishes to suit various architectural styles and personal preferences. They can be easily painted or laminated with different colours and textures, providing flexibility in achieving the desired aesthetic appeal. Moreover, WPC doors are available in various sizes, enabling customisation to fit specific door frames, unlike plywood doors that often require additional modifications for a perfect fit.

In summary, WPC doors clearly outshine plywood doors in various aspects. The convenience of installation, affordability, superior durability, moisture resistance, environmental sustainability, and aesthetic options make WPC doors the ideal choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
