Modern Living Room

Minimal. Sleek. Thoughtfully Designed.

A Modern Living Room is all about clean lines, neutral tones, and intelligent space planning. It combines comfort with contemporary aesthetics to create a clutter-free, stylish environment that's easy to live in and love.

Design Style: Contemporary Minimalist
Ideal For: Urban apartments, open-layout homes, and compact spaces

Key Features:

  • Floating TV unit with push-to-open drawers
  • Monochrome palette with pops of color
  • Concealed storage and cable management
  • False ceiling with recessed LED strip lighting
  • Low-profile furniture with sharp edges
  • Accent wall with texture paint or laminate paneling
Best Fit For: Homeowners who prefer sleek finishes, smart storage, and a timeless, modern vibe

Contemporary Living Room

Refined. Timeless. Effortlessly Elegant.

A Contemporary Living Room embraces simplicity, neutral tones, and clean design. It balances modern aesthetics with functional elements, creating a space that feels open, sophisticated, and always in style.

Design Style: Contemporary / Urban Modern
Ideal For: Apartments, villas, and upscale interiors with a clean, polished vibe

Key Features:

  • Neutral color palette with subtle contrast
  • Straight-line furniture with metal or wood accents
  • Modular TV unit with matte or gloss finishes
  • Textured wall panels or large-format tiles

Best Fit For: Homeowners who prefer modern design with timeless appeal — calm, clutter-free, and classy

Classic Living Room

Elegant. Timeless. Gracefully Detailed.

A Classic Living Room blends rich textures, warm tones, and intricate detailing to create a space that exudes charm and sophistication. It's perfect for those who appreciate traditional design, yet want a home that feels warm and welcoming.

Design Style: Traditional / Neo-Classical
Ideal For: Villas, heritage homes, or modern homes with a regal touch

Key Features:

  • Carved wood TV units and cabinets
  • Panelled walls with warm color tones
  • Antique brass or gold-finished lighting
  • Classic false ceiling with cornices or medallions
  • Chesterfield sofas or tufted seating
  • Decor elements like arches, framed artwork, and vintage rugs
Best Fit For: Homeowners who love timeless design, elegant finishes, and curated decor that never goes out of style

Open-Plan Living Room

Seamless. Spacious. Effortlessly Connected.

An Open-Plan Living Room blends the living, dining, and kitchen areas into one continuous, flowing space — ideal for modern lifestyles. It enhances natural light, encourages togetherness, and creates an airy, uncluttered vibe.

Design Style: Contemporary / Minimalist / Scandinavian
Ideal For: Apartments, villas, and homes with open layouts

Key Features:

  • Unified color palette across zones
  • Low partitions or modular dividers for subtle zoning
  • Floating TV units with sleek storage
  • L-shaped or sectional sofas to define space
  • False ceiling with layered lighting across zones
  • Decor accents that tie the spaces together
Best Fit For: Homeowners who want a multifunctional, free-flowing living space that blends comfort and social connection

Dreaming of a home where everything flows together beautifully?
