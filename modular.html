Parallel Kitchen Design

Smart. Symmetrical. Space-Optimized.

Parallel kitchens are perfect for compact to mid-sized homes, offering maximum utility with minimal footprint. This layout features two parallel countertops, ensuring a highly efficient work triangle and clutter-free space for cooking, prepping, and storing.

Layout Type: Parallel / Galley Style
Ideal For: Narrow or compact kitchens with high efficiency needs Storage

Storage:

  • Overhead Cabinets with Lift-up Shutters
  • Tall unit for appliances or pantry
  • Deep base drawers with organizer inserts
Best Fit For: Homes with linear kitchen walls on both sides

L-Shaped Kitchen Design

Open. Efficient. Easy to Navigate.

L-shaped kitchens are the go-to layout for open-plan homes, providing an effortless work triangle while keeping everything within arm’s reach. This design maximizes corner space and allows flexibility for dining counters or additional storage.

Layout Type: L-Shaped Kitchen
Ideal For: Corner kitchens, open-plan layouts, mid to large-sized homes

Storage:

  • Overhead Cabinets with Handleless Finish
  • Overhead Cabinets with Handleless Finish
  • Tall Appliance Unit
  • Corner Storage
  • Open Display Shelves
Best Fit For: Home chefs and families who want a clear zone for cooking, prepping, and cleaning — all with expansive storage.

U-Shaped Kitchen Design

Spacious. Seamless. Storage-Rich.

U-shaped kitchens are perfect for homeowners who love to cook and need everything within reach. This layout surrounds you on three sides, creating a highly efficient and functional workspace with generous storage and counter space.

Layout Type: U-Shaped Kitchen
Ideal For: Large to mid-sized kitchens with dedicated space for cooking

Storage:

  • Overhead Cabinets with Lift-Up Shutters
  • Dedicated Sink & Hob Zones
  • Deep Drawers with Tandem Boxes
Best Fit For: Home chefs and families who want a clear zone for cooking, prepping, and cleaning — all with expansive storage.

Island Kitchen

Bold. Modern. Multi-functional.

Island kitchens are the epitome of luxury and efficiency — combining prep, dining, and storage into one central hub. This layout is perfect for open-plan homes, making your kitchen the heart of family gatherings and social moments.

Layout Type: Island Kitchen
Ideal For: Spacious homes, open-plan layouts, contemporary interiors

Storage:

  • Full Wall Cabinets
  • Overhead Units with Ambient Lighting
  • Island Base Cabinets
  • Tall Appliance Wall
  • Hidden Cutlery & Utility Drawers
  • Decor Display Shelves
Best Fit For: Modern, open-concept homes that demand both style and high utility. Works best in large kitchens with free space around the island for easy movement.

Straight-Line Kitchen

Minimal. Compact. Made for Efficiency.

Straight-line kitchens are ideal for smaller homes, studio apartments, or single-wall spaces. Despite their simplicity, they offer incredible efficiency and can be customized with sleek, space-saving modules.

Layout Type: Straight / One-Wall Kitchen
Ideal For: Studio flats, 1BHKs, and compact rental homes

Storage:

  • Stacked Overhead Units
  • Base Units with Full-Depth Drawers
  • Built-in Tall Unit for Appliances
  • Cutlery Organizers and Pull-outs
Best Fit For: Individuals or couples in compact urban homes who want a clutter-free, elegant kitchen with modern essentials.

G-Shaped Kitchen Design

Spacious. Seamless. Super Efficient.

A G-shaped kitchen offers extensive storage and workspace with a wraparound layout and built-in peninsula — perfect for large, functional kitchens.

Layout Type: G-Shaped Kitchen
Ideal For: Large kitchens, open layouts, and homes with dedicated cooking zones

Storage:

  • Four-Way Countertops – Separate zones for prep, cooking, cleaning, and storage
  • Extended Peninsula Unit – Can be used as a breakfast counter or serving station
  • Deep Drawers & Tandem Boxes – For pots, pans, and cookware
  • Tall Units for Pantry & Appliances
  • Corner Units with Magic Corners or LeMans System
  • Overhead Cabinets with Spotlights or Loft Storage
Best Fit For: Spacious homes with an open or semi-open kitchen area. Great for families who love cooking and hosting.
