WPC stands for Wood Plastic Composite. It's a material made from wood fiber and plastic that offers the appearance of wood with enhanced durability, water resistance, and lower maintenance requirements compared to traditional wood.
Quality WPC material can be identified by checking its density, water absorption rate, flexibility, and surface finish. High-quality WPC should have uniform color, smooth texture, minimal water absorption, and should not easily crack or splinter under pressure.
